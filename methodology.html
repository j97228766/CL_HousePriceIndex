研究方法

CL 重複交易住宅價格指數的建構方式，以及其相較現有臺灣價格指數的優勢。

CL 指數之主要優勢

📍 更廣泛的地理涵蓋範圍Broader Geographic Coverage

產製臺灣14個地區的城市級指數，包括其他多數指數未涵蓋的小型城市（基隆、嘉義、雲林、屏東、花蓮、臺東）。

Produces separate city-level indices for 14 regions of Taiwan, including smaller cities and counties (Keelung, Chiayi, Yunlin, Pingtung, Hualien, Taitung) that are absent from most competing indices.

📋 逐筆交易的實價登錄資料Transaction-Level APR Data

使用內政部強制申報的實價登錄（APR）資料庫，所有公平交易均須申報。這消除了以仲介為基礎的指數（信義、國泰）中的選擇性偏誤，此類指數僅涵蓋上架物件。

Uses the Ministry of Interior's mandatory Actual Price Registration (APR) database — every arm's-length transaction must be reported. This eliminates the selection bias present in broker-based indices (Sinyi, Cathay), which only capture listed properties.

🔁 重複交易控制品質差異Repeat-Sales Controls for Quality

透過追蹤同一不動產的多次交易，指數自動控制不動產間未觀察的品質差異，避免簡單中位數/平均價格指標的組成偏誤。

By tracking the same property over multiple sales, the index automatically controls for unobserved quality differences across properties. This avoids the composition bias in simple median/average price measures.

🔬 四種獨立的辨識方法Four Independent Identification Methods

以四種嚴格程度遞增的策略配對房屋——從地址區塊配對到精確 GPS 加面積配對——提供其他指數所沒有的穩健性檢驗。

Properties are matched using four strategies of increasing stringency — from address-block matching to exact GPS + area matching — yielding a robustness check unavailable in other indices.

📅 更長的時間序列（自2000年起）Longer Time Series (from 2000)

透過將2012年前地方地政事務所收集的資料與2012年後的實價登錄資料接合，指數延伸回2000Q1，涵蓋兩個完整房地產景氣循環。

By splicing pre-2012 data collected from local land offices with post-2012 APR data, the index extends back to 2000Q1, providing two full real estate cycles for analysis.

🏢 房屋類型分層Property-Type Stratification

依房屋類型分別建構指數：住宅非出租（RN）、住宅出租（RR）及商業/工業（CI）。多數競爭指數僅涵蓋一般住宅。

Separate indices by property type: Residential-Non-rental (RN), Residential-Rental (RR), and Commercial/Industrial (CI). Most competing indices cover only general residential properties.

🔒 公平交易篩選Arm's-Length Filter

系統性排除政府徵收、關係人/家庭交易（從備註欄位辨識），以及含有未登記附加物件而虛增名目價格的不動產。

Systematically excludes government acquisitions, insider/family transactions (identified from the remarks field), and properties with unregistered add-ons that inflate nominal prices.

🏗️ 排除新建物Excludes New Buildings

排除首次出售的物件（屋齡 = 1年），與信義的方法一致，確保新建物溢價效應不會造成指數偏誤。

Properties sold for the first time (age = 1 year) are excluded, consistent with the Sinyi methodology and ensuring that "new-build premium" effects do not bias the index.

資料來源

此指數以臺灣內政部每季公布的實價登錄（APR）資料庫為基礎。自2012年8月起，所有不動產交易均須申報，使其成為目前最全面的臺灣住宅交易資料來源。

The index is based on the Actual Price Registration (APR) database (實價登錄) published quarterly by Taiwan's Ministry of Interior (MOI). Since August 2012, all real estate transactions must be registered, making this the most comprehensive source of Taiwan housing transaction data available.

原始資料檔案從內政部開放資料平台下載，依季度及城市代碼整理。每季釋出約包含150至250個CSV檔，涵蓋全部22個行政區的買賣、預售及租賃交易。

The raw data files are downloaded from the MOI Open Data Platform and organized by quarter and city code. Each quarterly release contains approximately 150–250 CSV files covering sales, pre-sales, and rental transactions across all 22 administrative regions.

變數建構Variable Construction

  • 交易日期從民國曆換算為西曆（加1911年）
  • Transaction dates converted from ROC calendar to Gregorian (add 1911)
  • 建材標準化為6類（鋼骨、鋼筋混凝土、磚造、木造等）
  • Building materials standardized into 6 categories (steel, reinforced concrete, brick, wood, etc.)
  • 屋齡計算方式：交易年份減去建築完工年份
  • Property age computed as transaction year minus construction completion year
  • 計算交易總價及單價的對數值供迴歸使用
  • Log transaction price and log unit price computed for regression
  • 新竹市+縣及嘉義市+縣透過將縣級觀測值重新編碼至市代碼予以整合
  • Hsinchu City+County and Chiayi City+County are consolidated by recoding county-level observations to city code

重複交易迴歸

核心概念是追蹤在資料中出現超過一次的不動產。當同一不動產在時間 s 和 t（t > s）各有一次交易時，交易對數價格差可控制所有固定的不動產特徵：

The core idea is to track properties that appear in the data more than once. When the same property transacts at times s and t (with t > s), the log price difference controls for all fixed property characteristics:

ln Pit − ln Pis = αt − αs + εits

時間固定效果 {ατ} 以 OLS 估計。t 期相對於基期 0 的價格指數為：

The time fixed effects {ατ} are estimated via OLS. The price index at time t (relative to base period 0) is then:

Indext = exp(α̂t − α̂0)

所有迴歸均使用 reghdfe（Correia 2017）估計，並加入建物面積、土地面積、房間數及樓層等特徵控制變數，以吸收同一不動產跨期銷售間的變異。

All regressions are estimated using reghdfe (Correia 2017) with hedonic controls for building area, land area, number of rooms, and floor number to absorb within-property variation across sale periods.

基期標準化Base Period Normalization

所有指數以 2012Q3 = 1.00 為基準，與強制實價登錄資料起始時間一致，並與其他臺灣指數相容以便比較。完整歷史走勢圖中，所有序列進一步重新標準化為 2001Q1 = 1.00。

All indices are normalized so that 2012Q3 = 1.00. This coincides with the start of the mandatory APR data and aligns with other Taiwan indices for comparability. For the full-history plots, all series are further re-normalized to 2001Q1 = 1.00.

最小觀測值門檻Minimum Cell Size

城市-季觀測值少於 27 筆重複交易的組合予以剔除，以避免不穩定的估計值影響指數。此門檻遵循重複交易指數建構的標準做法。

City-quarter cells with fewer than 27 repeat-sale observations are dropped to prevent unstable estimates from driving the index. This threshold follows standard practice in repeat-sales index construction.

四種房屋配對方法

本研究的重要方法論貢獻之一，是使用四種嚴格程度遞增的標準辨識跨交易的相同實體不動產，提供其他現有臺灣指數均無法提供的穩健性評估。

A key methodological contribution is the use of four progressively stringent criteria for identifying the same physical property across transactions. This allows a robustness assessment unavailable in any existing Taiwan index.

M1

地址區塊級配對 Block-Level Address Matching

以標準化地址區塊（移除單元/樓層識別符）分組不動產。最寬鬆的方法。約配對 109,000 筆獨特不動產，產生 628,000 筆交易。

Properties are grouped by standardized block-level address (removing unit/floor identifiers). Most permissive method. Approximately 109,000 unique properties matched, yielding 628,000 transactions.

M2

GPS 座標配對 GPS Coordinate Matching

以內政部登記的經緯度座標精確配對不動產。約 51,500 筆獨特不動產及 130,000 筆交易。需有 GPS 資料。

Properties matched exactly on latitude/longitude coordinates registered with the MOI. Approximately 51,500 unique properties and 130,000 transactions. Requires GPS data to be available.

M3

GPS + 四捨五入面積（±5 m²） GPS + Rounded Area (±5 m²)

GPS 座標加上四捨五入至最近 5 m² 的建物/土地面積。約 22,200 筆獨特不動產。減少跨交易間細微測量差異造成的配對錯誤。

GPS coordinates plus building/land areas rounded to the nearest 5 m². Approximately 22,200 unique properties. Reduces mismatches arising from minor measurement differences across transactions.

M4

GPS + 精確整數面積 GPS + Exact Integer Area

最嚴格：GPS 座標加上整數四捨五入的建物面積必須吻合。約 15,500 筆獨特不動產及 33,500 筆交易。最小化錯誤的不動產配對。

Most stringent: GPS coordinates plus integer-rounded building area must match. Approximately 15,500 unique properties and 33,500 transactions. Minimizes false positive property matches.

主要報告指數使用方法一（最廣泛涵蓋）作為全國及城市級指數。方法二至四作為穩健性檢驗，用於房屋類型分層分析。

The main reported index uses Method 1 (broadest coverage) for the aggregate national and city-level indices. Methods 2–4 serve as robustness checks and are used in the property-type stratified analyses.

指數建構流程

1

make_master_tw.do

匯入所有城市代碼的季度 CSV 檔。將中文變數名稱轉換為英文。處理舊格式（無標題行）及新格式（含標題行）CSV。依交易類型輸出主要 .dta 檔。

Imports quarterly CSV files for all city codes. Renames Chinese variables to English. Handles old (no header) and new (with header) CSV formats. Outputs master .dta files by transaction type.

2

make_estim_sample_tw.do

合併買賣及預售資料。套用公平交易篩選條件。轉換民國曆日期。建立特徵控制變數（屋齡、面積、房間數、建材、設施）。

Combines sales and presale data. Applies arm's-length filters. Converts ROC calendar dates. Creates hedonic control variables (age, area, rooms, material, amenities).

3

make_repeat_sales_tw.do

以四種方法辨識重複銷售配對。依城市 × 不動產類型執行 reghdfe 迴歸。提取時間固定效果係數並以 exp() 轉換為價格指數。

Identifies repeat-sale pairs using all 4 methods. Runs reghdfe regressions by city × property type. Extracts time-fixed-effect coefficients and converts to price indices via exp() transformation.

4

make_repeat_sales_pre2012.do

將相同方法應用於2012年前的資料集（local_building_clean.dta），並在2012Q3 接合點將結果指數接合至2012年後的序列。

Applies the same methodology to the pre-2012 dataset (local_building_clean.dta) and splices the resulting index to the post-2012 series at the 2012Q3 junction.

5

make_fulltime_annual.do

將季指數聚合為年頻率，並將2012年前後的年指數整合為自2000年起的連續序列。

Aggregates the quarterly series to annual frequency and merges the pre- and post-2012 annual indices into a continuous series from 2000 onward.

6

time_series_plot.do

合併 CL 指數與信義、國泰、安富及官方比較指數。標準化為2001Q1，並輸出適合發表的 EPS 圖表。

Merges CL indices with Sinyi, Cathay, AIFE, and Official comparison indices. Normalizes to 2001Q1 and exports publication-quality EPS plots.

樣本篩選標準

  • 僅納入建物+土地合併交易（排除僅土地或僅建物）
  • Include only building + land bundle transactions (excludes land-only or building-only)
  • 限於涉及恰好一棟建物及一筆土地的交易（排除大宗/組合交易）
  • Restrict to transactions involving exactly one building unit and one land parcel (excludes bulk/portfolio deals)
  • 剔除涉及 ≥ 15 筆地號或 ≥ 15 棟建物的交易
  • Transactions with ≥ 15 parcels or ≥ 15 units are dropped
  • 剔除政府買方交易（以買方身分證字號代碼辨識）
  • Drop government-buyer transactions (identified from buyer identifier codes)
  • 剔除家庭/關係人交易（透過備註欄位關鍵字搜尋辨識）
  • Drop family/insider transactions (identified from the remarks field via keyword search)
  • 排除1946年以前建造的不動產（結構品質疑慮）
  • Exclude properties constructed before 1946 (structural quality concerns)
  • 排除新建物（銷售時屋齡 = 1年）
  • Exclude new buildings (age = 1 year at time of sale)
  • 要求完整的交易及建築日期；剔除格式不正確的日期
  • Require complete transaction and construction dates; drop malformed dates
  • 剔除缺少 GPS 座標的觀測值（方法二至四所需）
  • Drop observations with missing GPS coordinates (required for Methods 2–4)
